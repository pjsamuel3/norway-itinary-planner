## Saturday 23 May — Arrival Night

Landing at 21:45 — priority is easy and close. Three options at/near Gardermoen:

### 🏨 Radisson Blu Airport Hotel ⭐ *(Host recommendation)*
Directly connected to the terminal via covered walkway — no taxi needed with tired kids and luggage. Reliable, comfortable, good breakfast. Perfect for early morning flights.
- 📍 [Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/?q=60.192577,11.095312)
- ☎️ +47 63 93 30 00

### 🏨 Radisson Red Oslo Airport ⭐ *(Host recommendation)*
More modern and design-led, ~5 min walk to the terminal. Stylish rooms — a step up in feel. Both Radissons are easy to walk to from arrivals.
- 📍 [Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/?q=60.189782,11.102034)
- ☎️ +47 67 02 41 00

### 🏨 Comfort Hotel Runway *(Budget option)*
Requires a shuttle bus (80 NOK/person) but significantly cheaper. Breakfast from 4am. Less convenient late at night with young kids.
- 📍 [Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/?q=60.193262,11.071843)
- ☎️ +47 63 94 88 88

> **Tip:** Given the late arrival with two kids, either Radisson is the path of least resistance. The Blu is marginally easier (terminal connection), the Red is slightly hipper. Check prices — May can vary.

## Tuesday — Oslo Day 1

### 🚲 E-bike through the city
Borrowing our electric bike — fits both kids on the back. Suggested route: Vigeland Park → waterfront → Opera House.

### 🌿 Vigeland Park
Free, open 24 hours. Gustav Vigeland's monumental sculpture park — enormous and perfect for kids to roam. One of Oslo's unmissables.
- 📍 [Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/?q=59.927029,10.700865)

### 🎭 Oslo Opera House
Walk up the sloping white marble roof for panoramic fjord views. Free to explore outside.
- 📍 [Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/?q=59.907489,10.753128)

---

## Wednesday — Oslo Day 2

### 🏔️ Holmenkollen Ski Jump
Take the T-bane (Metro line 1) to Holmenkollen. Ride to the top of the ski jump for extraordinary views over Oslo. Ski museum on site.
- 🕐 Open daily 10:00–16:00
- 📍 [Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/?q=59.965009,10.664657)

### 🌲 Hiking from Holmenkollen
Marked trails lead directly into Nordmarka forest. Easy, well-signposted — manageable for a 6 and 9 year old.

### 👁️ Grefsenkollen Viewpoint
Panoramic Oslo viewpoint, accessible by Metro. Pairs well with Teknisk Museum nearby.

### 🔬 Norsk Teknisk Museum
100+ hands-on exhibits — science centre, oil rig simulator, model railway, aviation hall. Outstanding for kids.
- 🕐 Tue–Fri 09:00–16:00 | **Closed Mondays**
- 🔗 [tekniskmuseum.no](https://www.tekniskmuseum.no/en/)
- 📍 [Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/?q=59.966936,10.783378)

### 🚶 Maridalsvatnet
Oslo's main reservoir — lovely easy lakeside walk, beautiful in late May. Very manageable for young kids.

## Thursday — Oslo Day 3

### ⚓ Aker Brygge
Oslo's main waterfront district. Great for a morning wander, coffee, and watching the harbour.
- 📍 [Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/?q=59.909958,10.725805)

### 🛁 KOK Floating Sauna
Wood-fired floating sauna at Aker Brygge — heat up, plunge in the fjord. One of Oslo's most memorable experiences.
- ⚠️ *Check kid policy when booking*
- 🕐 Open daily from 07:00
- 📍 [Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/?q=59.909271,10.726909)

### 🏰 Akershus Fortress
13th century fortress on the fjord. Free to enter, cobbled grounds, great harbour views. Easy walk from Aker Brygge.
- 🕐 Open daily 06:00–21:00
- 📍 [Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/?q=59.907586,10.737084)

### 🎨 Tjuvholmen
Adjacent to Aker Brygge — sculpture beach, Astrup Fearnley museum, great architecture.
- 📍 [Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/?q=59.907723,10.721885)

### 🚢 Fram Museum
Take ferry Line 91 from Aker Brygge. Board the Fram — the world's strongest wooden ship, used in Arctic and Antarctic expeditions. Immersive and excellent for kids.
- 🕐 Open daily 10:00–17:00
- 📍 [Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/?q=59.903381,10.699729)

### 🖼️ Munch Museum
Home of The Scream and thousands of Munch works. World-class building with fjord views. **Free entry on Wednesday evenings.**
- 🕐 Tue–Sun 10:00–18:00 (Wed–Sat until 21:00)
- 📍 [Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/?q=59.905904,10.755261)
